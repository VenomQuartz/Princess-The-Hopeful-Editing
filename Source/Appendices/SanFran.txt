Theme: The Wrong Kind of Rainbow

There's two ways to get a rainbow. If you look at the sky at the right time you will see seven colours travelling side by side. But if you split a beam of light with a prisim seven colours will diverge, then fade away. 

San Franciso is the second kind of rainbow. The Light that led the Radient to cultural and political promimence within a generation of the Release has started to diverge. As they travel further into their own pet issue and subcultures the Nobility are forgetting how to work togeather, they are even forgetting how to understand each other.
